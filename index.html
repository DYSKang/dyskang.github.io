Biography

I am currently a postdoctoral researcher in the School of Information at University of Michigan (UMich), working with Prof. Qiaozhu Mei. I received my B.S. and Ph.D. degrees in Computer Science from Hanyang University under the supervision of Prof. Sang-Wook Kim in 2013 and 2022, respectively. My research interests include graph data mining and machine learning, especially community detection and embedding on graphs and hypergraphs.
